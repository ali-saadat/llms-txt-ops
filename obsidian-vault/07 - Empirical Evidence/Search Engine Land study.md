# Search Engine Land study

> [!info] 10-site controlled test
> A/B style: comparable sites with and without `llms.txt`.

## Finding

No measurable citation lift on any of the 10 sites that adopted `llms.txt`. Controls (matched sites without llms.txt) showed comparable AI-search performance.

## Why this matters

Smaller sample but controlled comparison. Triangulates the [[SE Ranking study|large-sample observational]] and [[OtterlyAI study|crawler-log]] findings.

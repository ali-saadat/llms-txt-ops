# SE Ranking study

> [!info] n=300,000 domains, 2025
> The largest-sample llms.txt study to date.

## Finding

No statistical correlation between presence of `llms.txt` on a domain and that domain's:
- Frequency of citation in AI-generated answers
- AI-driven traffic
- Visibility in AI search results

## Why this matters

300k domains is large enough to detect even small effects. The absence of a signal at this scale is meaningful evidence.

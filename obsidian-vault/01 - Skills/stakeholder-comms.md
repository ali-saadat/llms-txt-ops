# stakeholder-comms

> [!tip] Default for ALL writing/drafting tasks
> Including vanilla "write the Jira ticket" without qualifier. Drafts emails, Jira tickets, memos in 13 languages.

## When this fires

Triggers: "write an email to my SEO lead", "draft a memo for leadership", "explain this to my CEO", "talk to stakeholders", "write the Jira ticket" (unqualified).

> [!warning] Jira disambiguation
> "Write the Jira ticket" (vanilla) → stakeholder-comms.
> "Deployment Jira" / "CI ticket" → [[deploy]].

## What it produces

- Professional emails (HTML formatting, color-coded prioritization, tables)
- Jira ticket bodies
- Memos for leadership
- Internal Slack/Teams updates
